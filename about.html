About Burier

The Time Capsule Diary

Burier Diary aims to serve as a unique and simple to use dairy keeping interface. The diary is novel in its time capsule ability. After submission, entries you make will be stored away an inaccessible for a certain amount of time. After that time is up the diary entries will become reaccessible.

Burier Diary also has mood analytic features. The systems will analyze the emotions in each entry and report back trends in your mood over time. Are you happy? Has you happiness changed over the last couple months? Visualization tools will display these analysis in simple and easy to understand formats.

Burier Diary also allows you to read entries submitted by other people. You can share your entries with friends and other diary users if you wish. If you don't want to, you entries can easily be kept private and for your eyes only
